How To Make Your Own Marauder's Map

Interested in making your own Maruader's Map with the location of your choice? Please fill out our form to receive instructions to create your own map! We're very selective over who we pass this knowledge to, so tell us all the glorious details of your best prank! (The more explosive, the better!)

Things To Consider When Making Your Map:


  • Location you want to plot - limited to just one place
  • Material - will it be made of parchment? cloth? stone?
  • Who's allowed to read it?
  • What will it be used for?
