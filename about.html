What is The Marauder's Map?

"Messrs
MOONEY, WORMTAIL, PADFOOT, and PRONGS,
Purveyors of Aids to Magical Mischief-Makers
are proud to present
-THE MARAUDER'S MAP"


The Marauder's Map was a magical document that revealed all of the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Not only did it show every classroom, every hallway, and every corner of the castle, but it also showed every inch of the grounds, as well as all the secret passages that were hidden within its walls and the location of every person on the grounds, portrayed by a dot. It was also capable of accurately identifying each person, and was not fooled by Animagi, Polyjuice Potions, or invisibility cloaks. Even the Hogwarts ghosts were not exempt.


Creation

The Marauder's Map was created by Remus Lupin (Mooney), Peter Pettigrew (Wormtail), Sirius Black (Padfoot), and James Potter (Prongs) while they were attending Hogwarts. The titles on the map refer to the nicknames of these men in their Animagus form, and, in Lupin's case, his werewolf form. The four created the map via the usage of the Homonculous Charm, a powerful and advanced charm that enhanced the map to track the movements of everyone in the castle, as well as a charm to insult Severus Snape.

The ability of Sirius, Peter, and James to become Animagi enabel them to explore the castle grounds by night undetected. The interior of the castle was mapped over time with the help of James' Invisibility Cloak.



Usage

The map is normally disguised as a blank piece of parchment. To view the map, one must tap it with one's wand and recite, "I solemnly swear that I am up to no good." The content of hte map will then reveal itself.

To hide the contents of the map so the parchment appears blank again, one must again tap it and recite, "Mischief Managed."

An added bonuse is that if the bearer of the map approaches the entrance to a secret passage protected by a password, the password will appear on the map (usually as a speech bubble that appears close to the dot representing the Map's holder.)
